How to Play

How to Play:


1. Select Your Golfers: Choose 6 golfers from the provided list located under the ‘Entry’ tab. Ensure that the total points of your selected players do not exceed 300.


2. Register Your Selections: Once you have finalised the 6 players for your team, click ‘Add’ to enter your team. If you wish to enter another team, click on the ‘Add Team’ button. You can edit and amend your team(s) until the entry deadline.

3. Scoring and Rules: The winner will be determined by the lowest combined score of their 6 players. If a player misses the cut or withdraws after starting, they will receive a score of 'field average' +3 strokes. Withdrawals before starting allow for replacements, provided the replacement hasn't completed more than 3 holes. Replacement requests should be emailed to jason@jasonkellygolf.com and ripples7@me.com. If no replacement is nominated, one will be made based on the initial pricing list, normally the first name below your selection.

4. Entry Deadline: Entries close at 23:59 on Wednesday, May 15th, 2024.


5. Entry Fee: The entry fee is £25 per team. Upon submitting your team(s) and making the payment, you will be entered into the competition. Confirmation emails will be sent on Thursday, May 16th, 2024.


6. Entry List: A full entry list will be posted at http://www.jasonkellygolf.com/MajorSweeps.


7. Payment: Submit the £25 entry fee to Sanatander, Jason Kelly. Account Number: 21152830, Sort Code: 09-01-27. BIC: ABBYGB2LXXX, IBAN: GB27ABBY09012721152830. Please reference your name. Alternatively, use Paypal to jason@jasonkellygolf.com. Ensure you select 'send to someone you trust' to avoid deductions.


8. Prize Fund: The prize fund will vary based on the number of entrants and will be communicated in the Thursday email update and on the website.


9. Questions: For any queries, email jason@jasonkellygolf.com starting from May 13th.


10. Updates: Receive daily email updates with the leaderboard, also available at http://www.jasonkellygolf.com/MajorSweeps and in-running on Sunday @jasonkellygolf on ‘X’ platform.


11. Email Issues: In case of email problems, visit the website for updates.

12. Financial Responsibilities: Entrants are responsible for all banking charges, payment fees, and foreign exchange costs, both for entries and winnings.

13. New Player Registration: New players must register through the website and agree to the Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and joining this Private Members Club by ticking the respective box. Registering on jasonkellygolf.com makes us a 'Private Members Club,' ensuring compliance with gambling
legislation. Returning players are already members.


14. Prize Distribution: The number of paid places will be disclosed in the email on Thursday, May 16th, 2024, and on the website.
